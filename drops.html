Background

With the success of Uber and Lyft recently going public, there is defintely a huge demand for this industry. Drops is an idea to capture this market with the USP of it being the first of its kind premium taxi services for the elite. For this project the research consisted of only a competetive analysis.

Persona

Going further through the research, I identified 3 personas with different backgrounds. Since the goal was to design user centric produts it was essential to answer the question - "Who am I desiging for"? This helped in understanding our target audience their motivations and requirements.

User Flow

Mapping out the user flow helped in visualizing the number of steps a user needs to complete specific task. It also gave an insight into the architecture of the app.

Wireframes

Once I had a clear vision of all the required screens I began translating them into rough sketches. I started out with the classic pen and paper route. Drawing boxes out helped in getting a better understanding of different layouts. I later converted them into low - fidelty Wireframes using Sketch.

Style Guide

A style guide helped keeping things symmetric and also made the design process much faster. For color I went with a mix of gold and yellow - which represents yellow (taxi) and the prestige of gold. Since simplicity was the mantra I went with just one font type - Proza Libre. I wanted a font which added a royalty vibe to the app which Proza Libre delivered.

Design

Once wireframe was finalized the next step was to add visual design. The final design was made in Sketch with the prototyping done in Principle. You can see the final app below.
